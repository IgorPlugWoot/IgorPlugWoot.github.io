About IgorPlugWoot

IgorPlugWoot started by Igorce9 in 2014. Initially the IgorPlugWoot was very basic, had only chat commands. in 2015 the IgorPlugWoot won a menu and several new features but continued not very advanced. in 2016 with the return of Plug.DJ, IgorPlugWoot had its refined menu and gained new features and also entered a new developer to team (Gabriel Possamai).
